1. Introduction

This Best Practice Guide is meant to illustrate BoD conversion rules and document best practice for all elements specified in rule set and CSS.

Our aim is to publish high-quality, well-structured and beautiful e-books that provide a satisfactory reading experience on multiple devices. Furthermore, devices, formats and specifications are developing and changing rapidly. Future automated adjustments to our ebook archive hinge upon consistent coding and well-structered files.

This guide is a work in progress. Feedback and suggestions are strongly encouraged. Please contact us at ebook@bod.de
